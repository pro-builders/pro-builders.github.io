Pile Foundation

Pile foundation is type of foundation in which columns of small cylindrical diameter are driven or cast into the ground. It is made up of concrete, timber or steel. This type of foundation is basically used for bridge type of construction. Pile foundation is done in the areas where the upper layer of soil is compressible or weak. Pile foundation is basically used when the soil below the foundation does not have sufficient bearing capacity to carry the weight of the structure into deep soil up to hard strata.


Advantages of Pile Foundation

  • Initial cost is low.
  • Easy to handle and construct.
  • Best suitable for friction pipes.

Disadvantages of Pile Foundation

  • Vulnerable to damage
  • Vulnerable to decay.
  • Its durability decreases if subject to wetting and drying.
