Combined Foundation

When two or more columns are close to each other and if there foundation are overlapping then combined foundation is constructed. Generally it is carried out on fields which have low soil bearing capacity. It is very economical when isolated footing columns are constructed over it.

Combined foundation is again sub divided into three categories such as:

  • Rectangular Combined Foundation
  • Trapezoidal Combined Foundation
  • Strap Combined Foundation
