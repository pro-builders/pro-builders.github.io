Deep Foundation

A deep foundation is a type of foundation that transfers building loads to the earth. The depth of the ground in deep foundation is above 3m. This is because; to construct high rise buildings it is necessary to go deep into the ground to provide necessary support to the superstructure and protects it from collapsing.

Deep foundations are further classified into four sub categories such as:

  • Pile Foundation
  • Pier Foundation
  • Compensated Foundation
  • Caisson Foundation
